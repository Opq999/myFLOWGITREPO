## What you'll get
You will learn to leverage AI tools for comprehensive market research and the creation of visual assets like graphics, presentations, and videos. This approach is effective for quickly gathering information and producing marketing materials, especially when starting with limited resources.

## Tools you need
*   **ChatGPT** (freemium) - For initial research, brainstorming, and content generation.
*   **Perplexity** (freemium) - For in-depth research and finding sources.
*   **Gemini** (freemium) - As an alternative or complementary AI for research and ideation.
*   **Canva** (freemium) - For designing graphics and presentations.
*   **Fliki** or **InVideo** (freemium) - For generating AI-powered videos.

## Steps
1.  **Initiate Market Research** - Use ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini to gather initial information about your market. The author doesn't share their exact prompt; a starting point:

    ```
    Research the current market trends for [your industry/product]. Identify key challenges, opportunities, and target audience demographics.
    ```
    You should receive a summary of market information.

2.  **Deepen Research with Specific Queries** - Ask follow-up questions to Perplexity or Gemini for more detailed insights or to find supporting data.

    ```
    Provide data and sources for the growth rate of [specific market segment] in the last 3 years.
    ```
    You should get more specific data points and links to sources.

3.  **Brainstorm Content Ideas** - Use an AI tool to brainstorm content based on your research. The author mentions this as part of the 30% not covered by AI. A prompt could be:

    ```
    Based on the market research about [your industry], suggest 5 blog post ideas and 3 social media campaign concepts.
    ```
    You should get a list of creative content ideas.

4.  **Design Graphics and Presentations** - Use Canva to create visual assets. The author uses it for graphics and presentations.
    *   Open Canva and select a template for your graphic or presentation.
    *   Add text, images, and branding elements.
    You should see your designed asset in the Canva editor.

5.  **Generate Video Content** - Use a tool like Fliki or InVideo to create videos. The author uses these for AI-generated videos.
    *   Choose a video template or start from scratch in Fliki or InVideo.
    *   Input your script or let the AI generate one based on a topic.
    *   Select voiceovers and background music.
    You should be able to preview your AI-generated video.

6.  **Refine and Finalize** - Review all generated assets (research summaries, graphics, presentations, videos) for accuracy and coherence. Make any necessary edits before final use.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free Tier Focus**: All mentioned tools offer functional free tiers suitable for starting out. For video, free tiers often have watermarks or limited export options.
*   **Common Pitfall**: Over-reliance on AI without critical review. Always fact-check AI-generated research and ensure your brand voice is consistent in marketing materials.
*   **Better Results**: Be highly specific in your prompts to the AI. Instead of "research the market," try "analyze the competitive landscape for sustainable fashion brands targeting Gen Z in Lagos, Nigeria, focusing on pricing strategies and online marketing channels."
