## What you'll get
You'll get a highly customizable AI tutor that adapts to your specific learning needs, style, and preferred communication. This approach works by leveraging advanced AI models to create a personalized educational experience, making complex subjects more accessible and engaging.

## Tools you need
*   **ChatGPT Plus** (paid) — Required for accessing the custom GPT-4 powered Mr. Ranedeer AI Tutor.
*   **Claude** (freemium) — An alternative AI model that can run the Mr. Ranedeer prompt, offering a large context window (100k tokens).

## Steps

1.  **Access Mr. Ranedeer AI Tutor** — Open your web browser on your phone or computer and navigate to the custom GPT link. Note that this requires a ChatGPT Plus subscription.

    Click this link:
    `https://chat.openai.com/g/g-9PKhaweyb-mr-ranedeer`

    You should see the Mr. Ranedeer AI Tutor interface load within ChatGPT.

2.  **Continue the conversation** — Once the custom GPT loads, you'll see a button to start or continue the conversation.

    Click the "Continue this conversation" button.

    The AI tutor will greet you, ready for your input.

3.  **Configure your learning preferences** — Before diving into a lesson, tell Mr. Ranedeer how you prefer to learn. You can specify your desired depth of knowledge, learning style, and communication type. The author suggests using a "Wizard" if you're unsure.

    You can either describe your preferences directly, for example:

    ```
    Set my depth to Highschool, my learning style to Visual, and my communication format to Textbook.
    ```

    Or, if you're not sure what to choose, you can ask the AI for help:

    ```
    Talk the Wizard 🧙‍♂️ here!
    ```

    You should see the AI confirm your settings or guide you through the personalization options.

4.  **Start your personalized lesson** — With your preferences set, you can now ask Mr. Ranedeer to teach you any subject. Be specific about what you want to learn.

    For example, to learn about a topic at your chosen depth:

    ```
    Teach me about the history of Nigeria, starting at a Highschool depth.
    ```

    You should receive a tailored lesson on your chosen topic, presented according to your configured learning style and depth.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier alternatives:** While Mr. Ranedeer is designed for GPT-4 (requiring ChatGPT Plus), the source mentions it also works on Claude-100k. Claude offers a free tier, which could be a viable alternative, though the '100k' context window might be a paid feature. GPT-3.5 also works but is noted to be less effective and concise.
*   **Common mistake:** Using GPT-3.5 or an older GPT-4 model without the Code Interpreter (if you were to copy-paste the raw prompt, which is not provided in the source excerpt). The custom GPT link automatically handles the correct model. Also, forgetting to configure your preferences can lead to less effective or generic lessons.
*   **Tip for better results:** Experiment with the personalization options. If a lesson isn't quite right, adjust your depth (e.g., Elementary, Highschool, Undergraduate), learning style (e.g., Visual, Verbal, Active), or communication format (e.g., Textbook, Layman, Story) until you find what works best for you. The "Wizard" option is a great way to explore these settings.
