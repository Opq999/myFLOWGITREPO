## What you'll get
You'll learn a structured AI-assisted workflow to design software features with clarity and intentionality. This process helps catch bad ideas early and ensures maintainable code by focusing on pre-coding thinking and AI-driven stress-testing.

## Tools you need
*   **write-a-prd (via AI Chatbot)**: This is described as an AI 'skill' or custom agent for structured interviews and PRD generation. It's likely implemented using a general AI chatbot like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. (Freemium)

## Steps
1.  **Free-form plan**: Start by writing a free-form document in plain language. Describe the problem, your initial solution thoughts, known constraints, and uncertainties. This document is for your eyes only, serving to externalize your thinking.
2.  **PRD via write-a-prd**: Use the `write-a-prd` skill (or a similar custom AI agent) to transform your free-form plan into a structured Product Requirements Document (PRD). The AI will explore the codebase (if applicable) and interview you about every aspect of your plan, resolving dependencies and stress-testing your design.

    The author doesn't share their exact prompt for `write-a-prd`; a starting point for a general AI chatbot could be:

    
    Act as a Product Manager. I will provide you with a free-form plan for a new software feature. Your task is to interview me to create a structured Product Requirements Document (PRD). Ask me about the problem, proposed solution, constraints, edge cases, and dependencies. Explore the design tree branch by branch.
