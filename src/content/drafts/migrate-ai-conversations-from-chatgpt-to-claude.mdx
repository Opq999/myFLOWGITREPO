## What you'll get
You will successfully migrate your AI conversation history and ongoing projects from ChatGPT to Claude. This approach ensures continuity in your AI-assisted work, allowing you to pick up where you left off without losing context, by leveraging Claude's capabilities.

## Tools you need
*   **ChatGPT** (freemium) - To export your past conversation data.
*   **Claude** (freemium) - To upload and continue your AI conversations.

## Steps
1.  **Cancel your ChatGPT Plus subscription** - Navigate to your ChatGPT account settings, find the subscription management section, and initiate the cancellation process. You should see an option to manage or cancel your subscription.
    *   **Expected Result:** Confirmation that your subscription is set to cancel at the end of your current billing cycle.
2.  **Request a refund** - Immediately after initiating cancellation, look for an option to request a refund for your current billing period. The author emphasizes this to avoid being charged further if cancellation isn't immediate.
    *   **Expected Result:** Confirmation of your refund request.
3.  **Export your ChatGPT data** - Go to your ChatGPT account's 'Data Controls' settings and select the option to 'Export Data'. This will initiate the process of compiling all your conversations.
    *   **Expected Result:** You will receive an email, typically within an hour, containing a link to download a zip file of all your ChatGPT conversations.
4.  **Download your exported data** - Access the email sent by ChatGPT and download the provided zip file containing your conversation history. Ensure you do this before your subscription officially ends.
    *   **Expected Result:** A zip file containing your ChatGPT data is saved to your device.
5.  **Access Claude** - Go to the Claude website (claude.ai) and log in or sign up for an account. If you are migrating from a paid ChatGPT plan, consider Claude Pro for similar features.
    *   **Expected Result:** You are logged into your Claude account.
6.  **Upload and continue conversations** - Within Claude, you can upload your exported ChatGPT conversations. The author suggests providing context to Claude and then continuing your projects, code, or writing as if you were still in ChatGPT.
    *   **Expected Result:** Claude is ready to receive your prompts, and you can begin pasting your previous work or asking follow-up questions based on your exported data.

## Original source
This workflow is based on a Reddit post by user /u/boomroom11, shared on February 28, 2026. The author details their decision to switch from ChatGPT Plus to Claude due to ethical concerns about OpenAI's business decisions, providing a step-by-step guide for others who wish to do the same.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free Tier Viability:** Both ChatGPT and Claude offer robust free tiers, making this migration accessible without cost. Claude's free tier is generally very capable for most users.
*   **Common Pitfall:** Forgetting to request a refund after canceling ChatGPT Plus. The author strongly advises this to ensure you are not billed further if the cancellation doesn't take effect immediately.
*   **Tip for Better Results:** When uploading your ChatGPT conversations to Claude, be explicit about the context. You can start a new chat with Claude and paste a summary of your project or the last few turns of a conversation, then ask Claude to 'pick up where we left off' or 'continue this discussion'.
