## Words you'll see, explained
*   **Generative AI**: Artificial intelligence that creates new content, such as images, text, or music, rather than just analyzing existing data.
*   **Model**: A trained AI program that performs a specific task, like generating images from text descriptions.
*   **Upscaler**: An AI tool that increases the resolution of an image while intelligently adding detail, making the image look sharper and more defined.
*   **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)**: A small, efficient add-on to a larger AI model that helps it generate specific characters, styles, or objects more consistently without retraining the entire model.
*   **Prompt**: The text instructions or description you give to an AI model to tell it what kind of image or content to create.
*   **4K**: A very high image resolution, typically around 3840 pixels wide by 2160 pixels high, offering significantly more detail than standard HD.
*   **GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)**: A specialized computer chip designed to handle complex mathematical calculations quickly, making it essential for running AI models efficiently.
