## What you'll get
You will get a personalized, week-by-week learning roadmap generated from a website's content. This approach works by iteratively breaking down complex information into manageable steps, adapting to your learning pace and preferences.

## Tools you need
*   **Runner H** (paid) - An AI agent platform used here to crawl websites and generate learning roadmaps.
*   **Google Docs** (free) - A word processor used to finalize and deliver the learning roadmap.

## Steps
1.  **Initiate the Roadmap Architect** - Start the Runner H agent with its specific role. The author's prompt sets the stage for the AI to act as a learning path architect.

    ```
    Runner H “Roadmap Architect”
You are my Autonomous Learning Path Architect. Your mission is to build a fully customized, iterative learning roadmap from a given site—never proceeding without explicit user approval at every step.
    ```

    You should see the AI acknowledge its role and prepare for the next instruction.

2.  **Provide Your Email Address** - The AI will prompt you to enter the email where the final roadmap should be sent. Respond with your email address.

    *Expected Input:* `[Your Email Address]`

    You should see the AI confirm receipt of your email and proceed to the next step, which involves asking for the website URL.

3.  **Specify the Website URL** - The AI will ask for the website you want to use as a source for the learning roadmap. Paste the URL of the website.

    *Expected Input:* `[Website URL]`

    You should see the AI begin to crawl the provided website, extracting its table of contents and nested sections.

4.  **Classify Topics by Difficulty** - The AI will process the extracted content and classify each topic as 'Beginner', 'Intermediate', or 'Advanced'. This helps in structuring the learning path.

    You should see a confirmation that topics have been tagged by difficulty.

5.  **Provide Your Learning Profile** - The AI will ask for details about your background to personalize the roadmap. This includes your experience level (on a scale, e.g., 1-5), your strong areas, your challenge zones, and your daily or weekly availability for learning.

    *Expected Input:* Respond to each question with your specific details. For example:
    `Experience Level: 3`
    `Strong Areas: Python, basic algorithms`
    `Challenge Zones: Advanced data structures, system design`
    `Availability: 60-90 minutes per day`

    You should see the AI calculate the total number of weeks needed and average hours per week based on your input.

6.  **Review and Approve Week 1** - The AI will present the plan for the first week, including up to 10 topics, each with a description, estimated time, and a clickable link. You will then need to approve this plan or request refinements before the AI proceeds.

    *Expected Action:* Reply with "Go Week 2" to approve, or provide feedback for adjustments.

    You should see the AI acknowledge your feedback or proceed to plan the next week upon approval.

7.  **Iterate Through Weeks** - Continue the process of reviewing and approving each subsequent week's plan. The AI will build the roadmap one week at a time, pausing for your explicit approval at each stage.

    You should see the AI present plans for Week 2, Week 3, and so on, until all topics are scheduled.

8.  **Finalize the Roadmap** - Once all topics are scheduled and you are satisfied, instruct the AI to finalize the roadmap.

    *Expected Input:* `Finalize Roadmap`

    The AI will then generate a Google Doc titled "My [SiteName] Mastery Roadmap", add the catchphrase "Roadmap Complete – Mastery Unlocked! 🔓", and email it to the address you provided earlier.

    You should receive an email with a link to your completed roadmap in Google Docs.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier alternatives:** While Runner H is a paid tool, the concept of using AI to generate learning roadmaps can be explored with free AI models like ChatGPT or Claude. You would need to manually scrape website content and then prompt the AI to structure it, which is more labor-intensive.
*   **Common pitfall:** Ensure you provide clear and specific details in your learning profile (step 5). Vague answers about your experience or availability can lead to a less effective roadmap.
*   **Tip for better results:** Be prepared to iterate. If the first week's plan isn't quite right, provide specific feedback to the AI. The iterative nature is key to tailoring the roadmap to your exact needs.
