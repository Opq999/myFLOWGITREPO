## What you'll get
You will create a series of unique, often surreal or "blursed" (blessed + cursed) images and a developing story, all generated by an AI. This approach uses the AI's ability to interpret and expand on visual cues, allowing you to guide a creative process step-by-step. It's excellent for brainstorming or creating imaginative art.

## Tools you need
*   **ChatGPT** (freemium): Used to generate images and text based on your prompts.

## Steps
1.  **Start with a core concept**: Open ChatGPT and provide an initial descriptive phrase for the AI to visualize. The author started with a character description from a film.

    ```
    Generate an image of a character with huge, muscular legs and a little noodle body.
    ```

    You should get an initial image and possibly a short description from ChatGPT.

2.  **Generate the first image**: Paste your concept into the chat interface and send it.

    ```
    Generate an image of a character with huge, muscular legs and a little noodle body.
    ```

    ChatGPT will create an image based on your description. This image will be the starting point for your story.

3.  **Iterate based on the image**: Look closely at the image ChatGPT just created. Describe what you see, or what new element you want to add, to guide the next generation. The author doesn't share their exact subsequent prompts, but the idea is to react to the previous image.

    ```
    Now, make that character ride a tiny bicycle through a bustling market.
    ```

    You should get a new image and/or story element that builds on the previous one.

4.  **Repeat and refine**: Continue this process, using each new image or story detail to inspire your next prompt. You can describe changes, add new elements, or ask the AI to continue the narrative.

    ```
    The market is full of strange, colorful fruits. Show the character trying to balance a stack of them while riding.
    ```

    You will get a series of images and a developing narrative that evolves with each step, creating a unique story.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier alternatives**: Other freemium chat models like Google Gemini (gemini.google.com) or Microsoft Copilot (copilot.microsoft.com) also offer image generation capabilities that could be used for a similar workflow.
*   **Common mistake**: Being too vague with your prompts. The more specific you are about what you want to see or change, the better the AI can follow your creative direction. Try to describe colors, emotions, settings, and actions clearly.
*   **Tip for better results**: If you're stuck on what to prompt next, ask the AI to describe the image it just created. This can give you new ideas for your next prompt. Also, consider using speech-to-text tools (like those built into your phone's keyboard) to quickly dictate longer, more descriptive prompts, as the original author did.
