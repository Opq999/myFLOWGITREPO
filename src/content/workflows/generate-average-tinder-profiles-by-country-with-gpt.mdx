## What you'll get
You'll get a detailed, realistic average Tinder profile for a specified country, complete with bio, interests, music, and descriptions of typical profile photos. This helps you understand cultural dating nuances or generate creative content ideas.

## Tools you need
*   GPT (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) - freemium

## Steps
1.  Open your preferred AI chatbot (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini).
2.  Use the following prompt, replacing `(country)` with your desired country:


Create a realistic average male/female Tinder profile from (country), including local stereotypes, dating-app clichés, bios, interests, music and typical profile photos.


3.  Review the generated profile, noting the cultural nuances and clichés included.

## Original source
This workflow was inspired by Reddit user /u/Vlaxilla, who shared their experiment of using GPT to generate average Tinder profiles from around the world on the Reddit platform.

## Notes & variations
Most major AI chatbots offer a free tier that can execute this prompt effectively. You can experiment with different countries, specify 'male' or 'female' more explicitly in the prompt, or ask for multiple profiles to compare regional differences.
