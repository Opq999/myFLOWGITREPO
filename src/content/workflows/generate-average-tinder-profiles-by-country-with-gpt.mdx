## What you'll get

A detailed, made-up "average" Tinder profile for any country you pick, with a bio, interests, music taste, and descriptions of the typical photos, leaning into local dating-app clichés. It is a fun way to explore cultural dating stereotypes or spark content ideas. (It is a playful exercise, not real data about real people.)

## Tools you need

- **A free AI chatbot** (freemium): ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini all handle this on their free tier.

## Steps

1. **Open your chatbot**: go to ChatGPT (or Claude or Gemini) and start a new chat.

2. **Paste the prompt**: swap `(country)` for the country you want, and pick male or female:

   ```
   Create a realistic "average" male/female Tinder profile from (country), including local
   stereotypes, dating-app cliches, a bio, interests, music taste, and a description of the
   typical profile photos.
   ```

   You should get a full profile reflecting the clichés of that country's dating scene.

3. **Vary and compare**: change the country, or ask for several profiles at once to compare regions. You can also ask it to make the bio funnier or more specific.

## Original source

Based on a post by Reddit user /u/Vlaxilla in r/ChatGPT, who used GPT to generate "average" Tinder profiles from around the world.

## Notes & variations

- **Free-tier viability**: every major chatbot's free tier runs this easily, with very little data.
- **Common pitfall**: forgetting to specify male or female, or the country, which gives a vaguer result.
- **Tip for better results**: add specifics ("a 25-year-old in Lagos", "into Afrobeats") to get a sharper, more recognizable profile.
