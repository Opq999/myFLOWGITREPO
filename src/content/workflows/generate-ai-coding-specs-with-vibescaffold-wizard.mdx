## What you'll get
You will generate a comprehensive set of documents, including a product vision, technical architecture, data models, and a staged development plan, all formatted to be understood by AI coding agents. This approach works by providing detailed upfront context and guidance to AI coding tools, leading to more accurate and actionable results.

## Tools you need
*   **VibeScaffold** (free) — A web-based wizard that guides you through defining your product idea and generating technical specifications for AI coding agents.

## Steps
1.  **Start the VibeScaffold wizard** — Navigate to the VibeScaffold website and begin the process.
    You should see the first step of the wizard, likely asking for your initial product idea.
2.  **Define your product vision and MVP** — Follow the prompts to describe your product idea and its Minimum Viable Product (MVP).
    The tool will guide you through articulating the core features and goals.
3.  **Generate technical architecture and data models** — The AI will assist in creating the technical architecture and data models based on your vision.
    You should see outputs related to how the product will be built and how data will be structured.
4.  **Create a staged development plan** — The wizard will help you break down the implementation into manageable stages.
    You will receive a plan outlining the steps for development.
5.  **Generate AGENTS.md for automated workflows** — The final step is to create a document that helps AI agents understand and execute the plan.
    You should get a file or text output ready for use with AI coding tools.

## Original source
This workflow is based on a tool called VibeScaffold, created by straydusk and shared on Hacker News. The author developed this wizard to streamline the process of translating initial ideas into detailed specifications that AI coding agents can effectively use for development.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free tier viability**: VibeScaffold is entirely free to use, making it highly accessible.
*   **Common pitfall**: Providing vague or incomplete information in the initial steps can lead to less useful technical specifications. Be as detailed as possible about your product vision and MVP.
*   **Tip for better results**: Actively review and refine the AI-generated outputs at each stage. The tool is designed to provide scaffolding, but your input is crucial for ensuring the AI coding agents receive the most accurate and effective guidance.
