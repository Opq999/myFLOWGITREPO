## What you'll get
You will get a new, modified invoice PDF generated directly on your phone, ready to send. This approach allows you to quickly handle urgent document needs using an AI chat assistant, even when you don't have access to a computer.

## Tools you need
*   **Claude** (freemium): An AI chat assistant that can understand documents and generate new ones based on your instructions.

## Steps
1.  **Open Claude and upload your last invoice**: Open the Claude app or website on your phone. Start a new chat. Look for an attachment icon (often a paperclip) and upload your most recent invoice PDF.

    You should see Claude confirm it has received the document, perhaps by summarizing its contents.

2.  **Ask Claude to recreate the invoice**: Type a prompt asking Claude to recreate the invoice.

    ```
    Recreate this invoice. I need to send another one fast.
    ```

    Claude will process the request. You should get a replica of your invoice, likely as a downloadable PDF or a text representation that can be converted.

3.  **Adjust the invoice details**: Tell Claude what changes you need to make to the invoice.

    ```
    Adjust the invoice number, billing period, and costs.
    ```

    Claude will likely ask for the specific new details (e.g., "What should the new invoice number be?"). Provide these details in the chat. Claude will then generate the updated invoice.

4.  **Download and send the new invoice**: Once Claude generates the updated invoice, download the PDF to your phone. Then, open your email app, draft your urgent email, attach the newly downloaded invoice PDF, and send it.

    You will have a correctly updated invoice PDF ready to send to your client.

## Original source
This workflow was shared by Reddit user /u/alp82, who described how they used Claude on their mobile phone to quickly generate and modify an urgent invoice while waiting in a roller coaster queue at Europa Park.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier alternatives**: Other freemium AI chat assistants like ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) or Gemini (gemini.google.com) might also be able to perform similar tasks, though their exact document handling capabilities can vary.
*   **Common mistake**: A common pitfall is not giving Claude enough specific details for the changes. If you just say "adjust the costs," it won't know what to change them to. Be clear, for example: "Change the cost for item A to NGN 50,000 and add a new item B for NGN 20,000."
*   **Tip for better results**: For best results, ensure your original invoice PDF is clear and well-formatted. If it's a scanned image, Claude might struggle to extract the text accurately. A digital PDF works best.
