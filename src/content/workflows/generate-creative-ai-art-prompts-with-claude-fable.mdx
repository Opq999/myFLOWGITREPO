## What you'll get

A simple way to dream up wildly creative AI art prompts: mash a character or theme together with a famous art style, and let the AI write a rich, detailed prompt you can drop into any image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion). The clever combinations are what make the images interesting. This uses Claude's Fable model, which is good at imaginative writing.

## Tools you need

- **Claude (Fable)** (freemium): the chat AI you ask for the prompts. The free tier works, within its usage limits.

## Steps

1. **Ask Claude to combine a subject and a style**: the winning pattern is "[character or theme] but in the style of [artist or art style], described in detail". For example:

   ```
   Generate a detailed AI art prompt that combines the characters from Aqua Teen Hunger
   Force with the painting style of Caravaggio. Describe the scene, composition, and
   lighting fully, ready to paste into an image generator.
   ```

   You should get a vivid, paste-ready art prompt.

2. **Review and refine**: if it is not quite right, ask for a different mood, composition, or more detail.

3. **Use it in an art generator**: paste the prompt into Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, or any AI image tool to create the picture.

## Notes & variations

- **Free-tier usage**: claude.ai's free tier covers this, with limits. If you hit them, wait, or try a free model like Gemini.
- **Experiment**: the magic is in the combinations. Mix unexpected characters, artists, eras, and moods, and be specific about composition and rendering.
- **Any art tool works**: the prompts Claude writes are generic, so they work in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, or others.
