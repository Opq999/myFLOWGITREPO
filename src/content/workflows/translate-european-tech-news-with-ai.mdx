## What you'll get

You will get a workflow to aggregate European tech news from RSS feeds, generate AI-powered summaries, and translate them into multiple languages. This approach helps you quickly process and understand global tech trends relevant to European markets, even if you don't speak all the languages.

## Tools you need

*   **AI (Large Language Model)** (freemium) - To summarize and translate news articles. Examples include Gemini, ChatGPT, or Claude.
*   **RSS Reader** (freemium) - To collect news from various European tech websites. Feedly is a popular choice.

## Steps

1.  **Identify and Add RSS Feeds** — Find the RSS feed URLs for European tech news websites you want to follow. Use your RSS reader to subscribe to these feeds.
    You should see the latest headlines from these sources appearing in your RSS reader.
2.  **Select an Article for Processing** — Choose an article from your RSS feed that you want to summarize and translate.
    You will have the article's title and a brief snippet or the full text ready.
3.  **Generate AI Summary** — Copy the text of the article and paste it into your AI tool. Ask the AI to summarize it concisely.

    ```
    Summarize the following European tech news article in 3-4 sentences:
    [Paste article text here]
    ```

    You should receive a short summary of the article's main points.
4.  **Translate the Summary** — Take the AI-generated summary and ask the AI to translate it into your desired languages.

    ```
    Translate the following summary into [Target Language 1] and [Target Language 2]:
    [Paste summary text here]
    ```

    You should get the summary translated into the languages you specified.
5.  **Review and Refine (Optional but Recommended)** — Read through the AI-generated summary and translations. If any parts seem unclear or inaccurate, you can ask the AI to rephrase or clarify specific sentences.
    You will have a more accurate and understandable version of the news in your target languages.

## Original source

This workflow is inspired by a project shared on Hacker News by user Merinov, who built a multilingual news aggregator for European tech. The original post detailed technical challenges and solutions related to image generation, sitemap growth, and automated translation pipelines.

## Notes & variations

*   **Free Tier Viability:** Most AI models offer free tiers that are sufficient for summarizing and translating text. RSS readers also have robust free plans.
*   **Common Pitfall:** Relying solely on AI without review can lead to inaccuracies, especially with technical jargon or nuanced topics. Always do a quick check.
*   **Tip for Better Results:** When asking for translations, specify the context if possible (e.g., "Translate this tech news summary for a business audience into German"). This helps the AI choose more appropriate vocabulary.
