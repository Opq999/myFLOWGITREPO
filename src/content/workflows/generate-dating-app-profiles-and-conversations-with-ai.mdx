## What you'll get
You'll learn how to use AI chatbots to help you write dating app profiles, come up with opening lines for matches, and respond to messages. This approach can help reduce anxiety around dating apps and potentially lead to more matches by making your profile and messages more engaging.

## Tools you need
*   **ChatGPT** (freemium): A popular AI chatbot for generating text, answering questions, and assisting with creative writing.
*   **Claude** (freemium): Another powerful AI chatbot known for its conversational abilities and text generation.
*   **Gemini** (freemium): Google's AI chatbot, useful for generating text and providing creative suggestions.

## Steps
1.  **Create a dating profile**: Use an AI chatbot to help you write your profile description. You'll need to provide some basic information about yourself and what you're looking for.
    *   Open your chosen AI chatbot (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini).
    *   Paste a prompt like this, filling in your own details:

    ```
    Write a Hinge profile for a 30-year-old architect who likes hiking and indie films. I want to sound witty, a little vulnerable, and genuine. Mention I'm looking for someone to explore new restaurants with and who doesn't take themselves too seriously.
    ```
    *   You should get a few options for your profile description that you can then edit and use.

2.  **Generate opening lines**: Ask the AI for clever ways to start a conversation with a new match based on their profile.
    *   In the same chat, provide details from your match's profile and ask for openers:

    ```
    Give me 5 clever opening lines for someone who mentions they love sci-fi and live in Lagos.
    ```
    *   The AI will provide a list of potential icebreakers.

3.  **Sustain a conversation**: Get help crafting responses to keep the conversation flowing and show empathy.
    *   Describe the situation and ask for advice on how to respond:

    ```
    I am chatting with someone who just said they had a stressful day at work. How should I respond to seem empathetic but not intense?
    ```
    *   The AI will suggest ways to reply that are supportive and keep the conversation going.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free tier alternative**: All the major AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) offer free tiers that are more than capable of handling these tasks. You don't need a paid subscription.
*   **Common mistake**: Relying too heavily on the AI without personalizing the output. AI-generated text can sometimes sound too perfect or generic. Always tweak the suggestions to sound like you.
*   **Tip for better results**: Be specific in your prompts. The more details you give the AI about yourself, your match, and the desired tone, the better and more personalized the output will be.
