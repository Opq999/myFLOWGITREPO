## Notes & variations

*   **Free Tier Viability:** Google Colab's free tier is excellent for getting started, but GPU availability and session limits can be a constraint for very long training runs. Consider paid Colab tiers or other cloud platforms if you need more consistent resources.
*   **Common Pitfall:** Forgetting to save a copy of the Colab notebook to your Google Drive before making changes. If you don't, your modifications will be lost when you close the tab.
*   **Tip for Better Results:** Experiment with different fine-tuning methods offered by LlamaFactory (e.g., LoRA, QLoRA) and adjust hyperparameters like learning rate and batch size. Smaller, well-curated datasets often yield better results than large, noisy ones.
