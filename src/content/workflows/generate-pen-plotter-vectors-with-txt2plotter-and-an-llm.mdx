## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier alternatives**: While the author uses Flux.2-dev, other open-source image generation models might be explored, though their suitability for clean vector output may vary. For prompt engineering, free tiers of popular LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini are excellent starting points.
*   **Common pitfall**: Directly using standard image-to-vector tracing tools (like Inkscape's trace function or Potrace) on AI-generated images will likely result in "hollow" lines or double strokes, which are unsuitable for pen plotters. The key is generating centerline data directly.
*   **Tip for better results**: Experiment with different "technical drawing" or "blueprint" style keywords in your LLM prompt rewrites to see how they influence the geometric coherence and line quality of the generated output.
