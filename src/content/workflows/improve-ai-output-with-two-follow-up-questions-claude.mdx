## What you'll get
You'll learn two specific questions to ask any AI, like Claude, at the end of a conversation. These questions help the AI identify its own uncertainties and reveal important information you might have overlooked. This approach works by prompting the AI to critically evaluate its own responses, leading to more thorough and reliable results.

## Tools you need
*   **Claude** (freemium): An AI chatbot that can answer questions and generate text. This workflow works with its free tier.

## Steps
1.  **Complete your main task with the AI**: First, use an AI chatbot like Claude to complete your primary task, whether it's drafting an email, brainstorming ideas, or summarizing information. Get to a point where you feel the AI has given you a complete answer.

    You should have a response from the AI that you are ready to review or use.

2.  **Ask about its uncertainties**: After the AI gives you its main response, paste this question to ask it what it's least confident about:

    ```
    What are you least confident about right now?
    ```

    The AI will list several areas where its information might be incomplete or uncertain. The author notes that about one in four times, one of these items is very important and needs further investigation.

3.  **Investigate identified uncertainties (optional but recommended)**: If the AI lists anything significant in the previous step, ask it to look into those points more deeply. For example, if it mentions a lack of recent data on a topic, you could ask:

    ```
    Please investigate [specific point of uncertainty] thoroughly and explain the root cause of the issue or clarify the point.
    ```

    You should get a more detailed explanation or a clearer understanding of the AI's limitations regarding that specific point.

4.  **Ask about missing information**: Once you've addressed the AI's uncertainties, ask this second question to uncover anything you might have overlooked:

    ```
    What's the biggest thing I'm missing about the situation right now? What don't I realize?
    ```

    The AI will provide insights or perspectives that you might not have considered, helping you get a more complete picture.

5.  **Review and refine**: Carefully read the AI's responses to both follow-up questions. Use this new information to refine your understanding, ask further questions, or adjust your original task.

    You should have a more robust and well-rounded output from the AI, with fewer potential blind spots.

## Original source
This workflow is inspired by a tip shared by Reddit user /u/call-me-GiGi on the r/ChatGPT subreddit. The author described how asking these two specific questions consistently improved the quality of their AI interactions.

## Notes & variations
*   This method works with most modern AI chatbots. If Claude's free tier isn't available or sufficient, you can use other freemium options like ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) or Gemini (gemini.google.com) with similar results.
*   A common mistake is to accept the AI's initial answer without probing deeper. These questions are designed to counteract that tendency. Don't skip them, even if you think the AI's first response is perfect.
*   To get the best results, be specific in your initial task. The clearer your instructions are at the start, the better the AI can identify its own weaknesses and your blind spots later on.
