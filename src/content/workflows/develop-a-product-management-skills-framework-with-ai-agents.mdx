## What you'll get
You will obtain a structured product management skills framework, designed to enhance your ability to frame problems, identify opportunities, and validate product ideas. This approach leverages battle-tested methods, enabling both you and AI agents to perform product management tasks at a professional level.

## Tools you need
*   **Claude** (freemium) - An AI assistant that can process and generate text, used here for understanding and applying PM frameworks.
*   **Codex** (free) - A tool mentioned for its integration with AI agents, implying a potential for programmatic application of the skills framework.
*   **GitHub** (free) - A platform to access and download the open-source product management skills framework.

## Steps
1.  **Access the Product Manager Skills Framework** - Navigate to the GitHub repository and locate the "Fastest Path" download link for the "pm-skills-starter-pack.zip". Download this file.
    You should see a ZIP file downloaded to your device.
2.  **Unzip the Starter Pack** - Extract the contents of the "pm-skills-starter-pack.zip" file.
    You will see multiple individual ZIP files, each representing a specific skill or set of skills.
3.  **Upload Skills to Claude** - Open Claude (web or desktop) and upload the individual skill ZIP files you extracted in the previous step. The exact method for uploading skills may vary depending on your Claude interface; refer to the `INSTALL-CLAUDE-DESKTOP.md` or similar documentation within the downloaded files if needed.
    The skills should now be available for use within Claude.
4.  **Initiate a Product Management Task** - In Claude, use a prompt to begin framing a product problem. The author suggests a starting point:
    ```
    Use the Product Manager Skills to help me frame this product problem.
    ```
    You should receive a response from Claude that begins to apply the framework to your problem.
5.  **Explore Framework Components** - Browse the other downloadable ZIP files (e.g., "discovery-pack.zip", "strategy-pack.zip", "delivery-pack.zip", "ai-pm-pack.zip") from the GitHub Releases page. Unzip and upload these to Claude to expand the framework's capabilities.
    You will have access to a wider range of product management tools and workflows within Claude.
6.  **Integrate with AI Agents (Advanced)** - For users with technical expertise, explore the "pm-skills-codex.zip" package and installation guides for Codex. This allows for programmatic use of the skills framework with AI agents.
    You will be able to automate product management tasks using AI agents trained on this framework.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free Tier Viability**: Claude's free tier has usage limits. For extensive use, consider upgrading or exploring other free AI models. The Codex package is free but requires technical setup.
*   **Common Pitfall**: Ensure you are uploading the *individual skill ZIPs* to Claude, not the entire starter pack ZIP. Incorrectly uploading the main pack may not make the skills available.
*   **Tip for Better Results**: When framing problems, be as specific as possible in your initial prompt to Claude. Provide context about the product, target audience, and the specific challenge you are facing to get more tailored and actionable advice from the AI.
