## What you'll get
You will get a realistic, documentary-style video featuring a young Korean woman in an authentic neighborhood, captured with an early-2000s consumer camcorder aesthetic. This approach works by using a highly detailed text prompt to guide the AI, ensuring specific visual styles, character consistency, and camera effects are included in the generated video.

## Tools you need
*   **OpenArt AI** (freemium): An online platform for generating AI images and videos from text descriptions.

## Steps
1.  **Go to OpenArt AI**: Open your web browser and navigate to the OpenArt AI website.
    *   You should see the OpenArt AI homepage. If you don't have an account, you may need to sign up or log in.
2.  **Access the video generation tool**: Look for a section or button related to "Video Generation" or "Text to Video."
    *   You should see an interface where you can enter a text prompt to create a video.
3.  **Enter your detailed prompt**: Copy the entire prompt below and paste it into the text input area for video generation.

    ```
    Main subject: young Korean woman, early 20s, natural everyday appearance, faded charcoal-grey sleeveless crop top, loose high-waisted light-wash jeans, black canvas sneakers, black cord necklace, black wavy hair in a messy side ponytail with wispy bangs. Realistic skin texture, minimal makeup, warm and approachable personality. Maintain consistent identity, clothing, hairstyle, and appearance throughout the entire video. Location: Authentic Korean residential neighborhood during a calm late morning. Narrow concrete alleys, low-rise homes, small terraces, potted plants, laundry lines, bicycles, utility poles, overhead wires, mature trees casting moving shadows, quiet residential atmosphere. No stores, advertisements, cafés, crowds, or commercial activity. Visual Style: Ultra-realistic documentary realism. Genuine candid behavior. Natural body language. Unscripted slice-of-life feeling. Strong environmental authenticity. Rich real-world details and believable human motion. Camera Style: Early-2000s consumer DV camcorder aesthetic. Friend casually recording everyday moments. Heavy handheld shake, imperfect framing, frequent autofocus hunting, lens breathing, exposure pumping when moving between sun and shade, occasional motion blur, subtle rolling shutter, mild digital compression artifacts, faded colors, soft contrast, slight sensor noise. No stabilization. No cinematic camera moves. No modern color grading. 00:00–00:02 Outside a small house entrance. She sits…
    ```

    *   The text input field should now contain the detailed description for your video.
4.  **Generate the video**: Look for a "Generate," "Create," or similar button and click it.
    *   The tool will start processing your prompt. This may take a few minutes. You should see a progress indicator or a message that your video is being generated.
5.  **Review and download**: Once the generation is complete, watch the preview of your video. If you are satisfied, look for a download button to save it.
    *   You should see a video that matches the detailed description, ready to be saved to your device.

## Notes & variations
*   **Free-tier limits**: OpenArt AI offers a freemium model, but generating detailed videos can quickly use up free credits. For longer or higher-quality videos, you might need to subscribe or purchase additional credits.
*   **Common mistake**: A common pitfall is using overly general prompts. The more specific you are about the subject, location, visual style, and especially camera effects (like "heavy handheld shake" or "autofocus hunting"), the better the AI can match your vision.
*   **Tip for better results**: To achieve even more specific outcomes, break down your video into shorter scenes and generate them individually, then stitch them together using a simple video editor. This gives you more control over each segment. Also, experiment with adding specific time markers for actions, as seen in the original prompt ("00:00–00:02 Outside a small house entrance. She sits…").
